Management system

TCIA has an in-house development team to design exclusive and unique tech solutions. Our immersive method allows the development of custom and specific systems for several market niches, even for businesses outside the logistics sector.

Our customers also have a permanent partner for the entire duration of the project in order to develop integrated solutions fully compatible with the ERPS options on the market.

SIAKI control tower

Smart monitoring using integrated warehouse management system

Unlike regular control towers, SIAKI (developed by TCIA) is a high technology system made for managing resources and channels in supply chain, specifically complex items (e.g primarily in the telecommunications). Our tool is easily integrated to ERPS and fully customized to meet all the needs of each business.

Specialist in the control and automation of stocks and resources for the logistics area.
